# Flag Explorer: Product Requirements Document

## Core Loop
1. **World Map Hub**: Interactive 3D/2D globe where children select a country (initially grayed out).
2. **Learning Phase**: 3-second intro showing the country on the globe with bilingual audio: "México... Mexico".
3. **Paint Station**: A blank silhouette of the flag appears. 
    - **Smart Brush**: Child swipes to color sections.
    - **Color Selection**: Only the correct flag colors are available in the palette.
    - **Bilingual Audio**: Tapping a color says the name: "Verde... Green".
4. **Celebration**: Upon completion, flag waves, sparkles appear, and a "Passport Stamp" is awarded.
5. **Quiz Phase**: After 10 flags, a quiz reviews them.
    - **Audio-Visual Options**: Since children may not read, options feature speaker icons and flag/shape thumbnails.

## Progression & Gamification
- **Passport**: Collection of stamps from completed countries.
- **Ranks**: Beginner -> Explorer -> Captain -> World Expert.
- **World Map Progress**: Map lights up in color as flags are mastered.

## Accessibility & Safety
- Full voice-over (Bilingual: Spanish then English).
- Large touch targets.
- No ads, no text required for navigation.
- Offline mode support.
